About

Research focus and background.

I study the interactions between snow, weather, and clouds in mountains. My research integrates field observations, satellite data, and numerical modeling to understand atmospheric rivers, snow dynamics, and their cascading effects on avalanche risk and hydrology. Beyond research, I teach and train others on snow science, avalanche awareness, and weather forecasting.
